Student Administrative Department

Student Administrative Department :
Is one of the organizations to promote morality, ethics and morality, desirable values for Students, so that all students have knowledge, ability, courage express what is right liability in duty have good values, can control themselves to live in society happily, behave as a good child to parents, guardians, goos disciples to the teacher and good person of the country. The administration has organized projects, activities to promote morality, ethics and desirable values as follows :



Sacred Heart of beauty according to the rules project :
The purpose of this project is to train and cultivate the correct values chart for all students to be come a person who has well behaved, good manners, respect the rules of the school, have a sense of responsibility for self and society, behave as a role model for the general public. With the management of the department, parents and class leaders will have a meeting to train students every week for students to learn and get to know the rules and regulations of the school that you should know and apply them correctly.



From the Heart..For you project :
Is a project to change attitudes, values and behaviors of students to be in a right track, and make a contribution to the society for students to see self-worth from doing good.



Sacred Heart's Beauty Culture project :
Beautiful Culture Project according to the Sacred Heart Let students learn and practice manners according to traditions. and the beautiful culture of Thailand will be practiced during the students' meeting in each grade level and in the period after respecting Thai National Anthem.



Student Inspector :
To strengthen leadership potential for student inspectors to have working skills to work in stepwise, have a teamwork, good leadership and follower traits and encourage Students can work with others. The student inspector will select from the primary school students, Grade 2 to Grade 6, selected from classmates to take care of dress discipline being late to school Rules for walking in lines of friends, etc.



Recording of Well Behaving Students :
Select students who have good behavior, morality, ethics and honor good people, by awarding good students certificates to be a role model and inspiration to others such as Dekdee Sri Phraharuthai of the Month Good boy Sri Phraharuthai, good boy, school in the Sacred Heart group and good children, Khlong Toei District Cultural Council



Honesty Promotion :
To encourage students and school personnel who are collecting money or valuables things will be honored and given Honor Certificate to give encouragement to continue to do good deeds and be an example for others.



Security and Discipline Check :
Organize teachers to take care of the safety and discipline of students in various areas of schools to ensure the students are safe from accidents and hazards that may arise from various causes such as crossing the road, playing, or from outsiders, etc.



Anti-Vices :
Organize activities to encourage students to have knowledge and understanding about the dangers of various vices; and Know how to properly protect yourself from all kinds of vices, by allowing students to participate in organizing activities against vices in various forms such as World Tobacco Day and World AIDS Day that will be performed as a play on stage after respecting Thai National Anthem. Organize knowledge bots, bingo games, guessing games, and throwing things into the basket, which every game will be giving knowledges about cigarettes and aids.And also organized a drawing contest on the Anti-Drug Day too. stuck
