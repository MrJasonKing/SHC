Basic information of Phraharuthai Convent School

 Phraharuthai Convent School is the first school of Faculty in Bangkok. Established on 28th February 1934 by Abbot Seraphine de Marie

 received the approval of Ponte Rene Perros head of Catholic Mass in Bangkok.Received a license to extablish a School on 10th January

 1937.Now Phraharuthai Convent School located at 94 Sunthonkosa Road, Khlong Toei Subdistrict, Khlong Toei District, Bangkok 10110

         

  School vision : Phraharuthai Convent School aiming to develop towards early childhood and basic education and educational standards

                according to the heart and mind of the lord Jesus ready to develop to be international on the basis of being Thai

                according to the religious principles of the learners for a complete human being


  Identity      : Love Humble Honest and Merciful

  Unique        : Languages and technology

  Philosophy    : Honesty Mercy Intelligence Love and Morality


  Love                 refers to the characteristics of a person who has patriotism, religion, monarchy.Including loving and treating 
                         others like self-love according to religious principles.


  Honesty              refers to the qualities of the mind and conduct that are honest and sincere.Always stick to the truth


  Merciful             refers to the qualities of the mind that wish others to be happy. Have love with a tender heart sacrifice 
                         forgiveness, accept others and be friendly with everyone.


  Academic excellence  refers to the characteristics of a person who is diligent in education. Until reaching the highest goal of life

                         or being successful is recognized by the society.


  Uphold the virtues   refers to the behavioral characteristics that show the stability of the mind. Who pursue virtue, adhere to the

                         religion and moral discipline of society.


         

  Motto : Initiative Intelligence Gratitude and Unity


  Initiative      means knowing how to start thinking innovating new knowledge and being a leader in doing goos and right things.

  Knowing         means knowing and seeking knowledge in various fields. So that they will have extensive knowledge for the benefit

                  of their own and social development.

  Gratitude       means knowing and appreciating the good or support that other people have for themselves and always wishing to repay

                  you.

  Unity           means knowing how to work together unity and reconciliation, unity in working for the common good, respecting

                  differing opinions to acheive effective work.

  School flowers Karawak   :Kraawak is a native Thai plant that looks like a climbing vine, the meat is large with hook-shaped hands

                            protruding from the vine around the young shoots are green. When the vines are brown the leaves are very

                            densely packed. It is a single leaf that leaves alternately along the joints in an oblong or rounded shape

                            Karawak flowers will emerge at the base of the leaves. The flower is a single flower with a hand on the

                            island.When the flowers are first out they are green and gradually change to yellow in 1 flower with 

                            petals. Which is divided into 2 layers, 3 petals each and 3 sepals. The flowers are small, thick and hard.

                            petals are oval in shape. The flowers are fragrant in the evening to late evening.

  Karawak convey the meaning of morality referring to personnel who are constantly developing themselves acting with love and

                     sacrifice with dedication to doing good and kindness without expecting anythign in return being gentle and kind,

                     being a lady and gentleman.
